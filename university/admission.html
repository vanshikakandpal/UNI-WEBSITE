Admission at Princeton University

Explore how to join one of the world’s leading institutions of learning and research.

Undergraduate Admission

Princeton offers a highly selective undergraduate program, admitting students who demonstrate exceptional academic achievement, leadership, and commitment to service.

  • Application Deadline: January 1
  • Required: Common Application or Coalition Application
  • Supplement: Princeton-specific essay questions
  • Standardized Testing: SAT/ACT (optional for certain years)
  • Financial Aid: Need-based, full demonstrated need met

Graduate Admission

Graduate admission at Princeton is offered through individual departments. Applicants are evaluated on academic excellence, research potential, and fit with the department’s strengths.

  • Application Deadline: December 1 (varies by department)
  • Required: Online Application, Statement of Purpose, Transcripts
  • Recommendations: 3 Letters of Recommendation
  • GRE: Required for some departments
  • Funding: All admitted Ph.D. students receive full financial support

International Students

Princeton welcomes students from around the globe. International applicants follow the same process as U.S. students, with additional requirements for proof of English proficiency (TOEFL/IELTS).
